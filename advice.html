A few things you should know:

  • As a freshman, you won't take AP classes. This is totally fine.
  • Don't worry about college yet - just join clubs or teams that you genuinely want to join.
  • Joining clubs or teams is a great way to make friends.
  • As long as you keep up with homeworks and pay attention in class, you'll be fine.
  • Your teachers will try to help you - let them know if you're struggling.
